## üì± Image Specifications

### **Recommended Dimensions:**
- **Width**: 1920px (minimum 1200px)
- **Height**: 1080px (minimum 800px)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 4:3
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Quality**: High (80-90%)

### **File Naming Convention:**
```
destination-name-description.jpg

Examples:
- taj-mahal-sunrise.jpg
- varanasi-ghats-evening.jpg
- ziro-valley-rice-fields.jpg
- dawki-crystal-river.jpg
- living-root-bridge-meghalaya.jpg
```

### **File Size:**
- Target: 200-500 KB (after optimization)
- Maximum: 1 MB

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Use landscape orientation** for most destination photos
2. **Include landmarks** in the frame for instant recognition
3. **Choose vibrant colors** that represent the destination
4. **Avoid generic stock photos** - seek authentic, location-specific images
5. **Mix daytime and sunset** shots for variety
6. **Show activities** where relevant (rafting, trekking, etc.)
7. **Consider seasons** - show destinations in their best season
8. **Local elements** - include people, culture, food where appropriate
